Meet Dr. Sara

Trained at NYU.
Built for this community.

Dr. Sara Khoshroozeh holds two Doctorate of Dental Surgery degrees — from Tehran University and from NYU College of Dentistry, where she graduated top of her class in 2013. She took over this practice with a simple mission: bring the highest level of care to Woodland Hills, and treat every patient like family.

Her patients know her for one thing above all else: honesty. She doesn't recommend treatment you don't need, she explains everything clearly, and she's available when it matters.
