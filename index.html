Meet Dr. Tabesh

Dentistry is not
just a job for her.

Dr. Farnoosh Tabesh founded this practice from scratch in Toluca Lake — building every relationship personally, one patient at a time. She graduated cum laude from the University of Maryland School of Dentistry and completed an advanced residency in General Dentistry before relocating to Southern California.

Her philosophy: treat every patient like your best friend, never rush, never oversell. Her team has been with her since the beginning.
